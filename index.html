About us

Here at GO!gym, our goal is to help you reach your goals. By eliminating the most common gym-related excuses, we've made it easier than ever to get yourself through the door and smashing your workout.

No time? No Money? No plan? No problem!

All our gyms are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week with a wide range of equipment and calendars chock-full of classes ranging from beginner to advanced, 15 minutes to 45! We also offer a free 1-week trial and a choice of membership options, both with no tie-ins or joining fee. Included in your membership is a free induction session, guided by one of our friendly on-site trainers, they'll answer any questions you have and guide you in the right direction to meet your fitness goals.
